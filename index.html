About Me

I have always held a keen interest in the world of development. I first discovered the joys of coding in 2010 where I experiemented with languages such as VB.Net, HTML and CSS. As the years went on, I slowly progressed through self-educatation and started to teach myself PHP.

When I stated with PHP, I realised that the best way for me to learn would be to have an actual project; that way I could learn certain aspects as I developed my project. With this in mind, I started 'Arappnid', a tarantula keeper's web application that tracked details such as last feeds, last moults and temperatures.

Sadly, I hit a hurdle a few months into my journey and the project ended up on the back-burner and my interest in coding began to fade as my frustrations grew. I didn't code for a while and my project ended up lost and forgotten.

Fast-forward a year or so and I saw the Code Nation Devlop course (3-weeks) and applied. I had forgotten all about it when Jake rang me, inviting me on to the course. I finished the 3-week course and found I thrived on actual tutelage. I am here to build upon the knowledge that I garnered thoroughout those 3 weeks. My goal is to work in a devlopment environment.
